# VeloBot 🚴

AI-powered Telegram bot for urban cyclists.

## What it does
- Daily ride/trainer decision based on weather + road conditions
- Considers your personal ride time windows
- AI coaching toward your cycling goal
- Progress tracking toward distance/speed targets
- Connects to Strava automatically

## For who
Urban cyclists who want to know:
**"Can I ride today?"** — without checking 3 apps.
